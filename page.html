CONSEQUENTIAM Proposal


My proposal for CONSEQUENTIAM innovation program was a module that is able to detect and estimate the number of mosquitoes in a closed room.

Knowing that mosquitoes are a great vector of tropical diseases like malaria, the goal is to warn the inhabitants when their environment is not safe.

The kinect technology will be featured in this module, and there will be three states: The "safe" state, the "warning" state and the "danger" state. When in the danger state, the module will send a notification to the user's phone to advice him the use of mosquito repellents in order to return to the "safe" state.

The conception is still underway, and I hope the prototype will be presented very soon.
